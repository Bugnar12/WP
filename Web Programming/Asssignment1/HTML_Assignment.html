History

The zoo was founded in 1975 and has been a popular attraction for families and tourists ever since. The zoo is home to over 1000 animals and has a wide variety of species. The zoo is also involved in conservation efforts and has a breeding program for endangered species.

Visiting hours

The zoo is open every day from 9am to 6pm. The zoo is closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day. The zoo is also open on public holidays.

Opening hours might differ if public holidays are occuring.
